Damage

How many Hit Points the opponent loses on each attack.

Armor

How much damage is blocked on each attack by the opponent.

Regeneration

How much hp is regained every regen cycle.

Attack Speed

How many ticks it takes for the next attack.

Victory Heal

How much hp you regain upon the end of a combat.

Dodge

Chance the opponent's attack does no damage.

Accuracy

Subtracts (or adds) to the opponent's dodge chance.

Lifedrain

Proportion of damage dealt which is reabsorbed as health.

Thorn

Damage dealt to opponent on taking damage.

  • Bypasses armor

Shatter

Chance to break 1 of your oppponents armor on attack. Armor regained after combat.

  • 15 Shatter means guaranteed -1 and a 50% chance for -2

Bleed

Chance to remove 1 of the opponents regeneration on attack. Regeneration regained after combat.

  • 150% Bleed means guaranteed -1 and a 50% chance for -2

Tear

Amount removed of opponent's max HP on attack. Does NOT restore after combat.

  • Applied after normal attack
  • Bypasses armor

Antiheal

Removes a flat amount of regen from opponent at start of combat.

Super Armor

Additional armor which blocks thorn and tear.
